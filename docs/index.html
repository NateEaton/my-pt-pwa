What is My PT?

My PT is a privacy-focused Progressive Web App for tracking physical therapy exercises and maintaining consistent rehabilitation routines. All data stays on your deviceâ€”no server, no tracking, no accounts.

This app is intended as an additional aid beyond those provided by medical professionals. The audience is primarily people with recurring or chronic physical issues that require long-term physical therapy regimen, continued at home beyond when they are under the direct care of a therapist.

Privacy First. Always.

Your health data is yours. My PT is a "local-first" app, meaning all data is stored directly on your device using IndexedDB. No server communication, no accounts, no tracking, no analytics. Your exercise data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it for backup purposes.

About This App

Developed by a retired hobbyist (with help from AI) for personal use during physical therapy recovery, this app is and always will be free, with no ads or tracking. It's a personal project shared in the hope that it helps others maintain consistent rehabilitation routines.
